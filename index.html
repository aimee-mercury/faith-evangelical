About Faith Evangelical Church

Faith Evangelical Church is a welcoming family of believers. We gather weekly to worship Jesus, grow through God's Word, and serve our neighbors with compassion. Whether you're exploring faith or seeking a church home, you are welcome here.

Since our founding in 1996, we've been committed to preaching the Gospel in every sermon and creating a community where people can grow in their relationship with God.
